# Kababayan MVP Scope Boundaries

## Included

- Dedicated marketing landing page
- Login and signup placeholders
- Guest entry into the application
- Community-tab architecture with Batangas as the functional MVP community
- Six Batangas community experiences
- Five-of-six completion plus typed closing reflection
- Certified Batangueño community badge
- Two Batangas place quests
- Private photo attachment or written accessibility alternative
- Place stamps
- Passport with guest identity, badges, stamps, photos, and reflections
- Local-only persistence

## Excluded

- Real authentication and password collection
- Backend or cross-device synchronization
- Maps, directions, transportation, and itinerary planning
- Reservations, ratings, reviews, and deals
- GPS and AI verification
- Public photo galleries or social feeds
- Destination search and recommendation engines
- Points, ranks, streaks, levels, and leaderboards
- User-created quests
- Merchant and administration dashboards
- Push notifications

## Ownership rule

- Discover owns community belonging.
- Journey owns meaningful location activities.
- Passport owns personal identity and memory.

Features must not duplicate or blur these responsibilities.

## Change test

Every proposed feature must answer:

> Does this directly help a visitor participate respectfully in Filipino community life?

If the answer is no—or another travel product already serves it—it is outside the MVP.
